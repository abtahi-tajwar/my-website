E-Commerce Platform

A full-featured e-commerce solution with payment integration, inventory management, and analytics dashboard. Built for scalability and performance.

6 months
Team of 5
100k+ Active Users
React

01. The Challenge

Our client, a rapidly growing retail business, was struggling with their legacy e-commerce platform that couldn't handle increasing traffic and feature demands. The system frequently crashed during peak hours, had a clunky checkout process leading to 65% cart abandonment, and lacked real-time inventory synchronization across multiple warehouses.

Key pain points included:

  • → Poor performance during high traffic periods

02. The Solution

We designed and built a modern, scalable e-commerce platform using a microservices architecture. The solution was built with React for a responsive frontend, Node.js for backend services, and PostgreSQL with Redis for data management and caching.

Modern Architecture

Implemented microservices architecture allowing independent scaling of payment processing, inventory management, and user authentication services. Used Docker and Kubernetes for containerization and orchestration.

03. Results & Impact

The new platform exceeded all expectations and delivered significant improvements across all key metrics:

40%
Faster Page Load

"The new platform has completely transformed our online business. We can now handle Black Friday traffic without breaking a sweat, and our customers love the seamless shopping experience. This investment has paid for itself many times over."

— Sarah Johnson, CEO
